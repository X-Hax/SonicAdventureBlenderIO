About Sonic Adventure I/OðŸ”—︎

The Sonic Adventure I/O Blender Addon, or short "SAIO", is an addon for Blender, adding the ability to import and export 3D related file formats managed by the Sonic Adventure Toolset (SATools).

The addon is a free open source tool developed primarily by Justin113D, with major assistance of ItsEasyActually, as well as smaller contributions by members of the X-Hax Sonic modding group. A tool for modders, by modders!
